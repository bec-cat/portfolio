Role: User Research, Problem Research,
Prototyping & Usability Testing, Wireframes, Pitching

Timeline: 12 weeks (Aug-Nov 2021)

Tools: Balsamiq, Adobe XD, Miro, Protopie

Overview

Grim Repair is a mobile application that is designed to simplify the process of repairing electronic devices & offer an end-to-end service including pickup, tracking & delivery.

This was created as part of a uni capstone course in a group of 5 people, with the objective of supporting people through the use of technology to improve the quality time at home.
